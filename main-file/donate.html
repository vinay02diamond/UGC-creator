Join My Small Business Community

My mission is to uplift small business owners who are just starting and help them grow. I travel directly to small businesses to showcase their products, provide equipment, host giveaways, and organize meet and greets. I want every small business owner to feel supported, seen, and connected to a community that cares.

How Your Support Helps

When you donate, you help me provide:

Content Equipment

Lights, tripods, backdrops, and props like flowers and small decorations to make their products shine

Giveaways

Items to help small businesses reach new customers and create excitement

Startup Assistance

Support for inventory, products, or tools small businesses need to get started or grow

Education and Training

Guidance on marketing, content creation, and business growth

Community Events

Meet and greets to connect entrepreneurs, share experiences, and build lasting support networks

Every donation helps me give small business owners the tools, guidance, and opportunities they need to succeed.
